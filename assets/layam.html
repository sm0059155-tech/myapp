About Sapta Tala System

The Sapta Tala system consists of 7 fundamental talas, each with 5 jati variations, creating 35 possible talas.

Angas: Laghu (variable), Dhrutam (2 beats), Anudhrutam (1 beat)

Gati/Nadai: Subdivisions of beats (3, 4, 5, 7, or 9)

Laya: Speed - Vilambita (slow), Madhya (medium), Druta (fast)

Total variations: 35 talas × 5 gatis × 3 layas = 525 possibilities
